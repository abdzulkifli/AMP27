# AMP27
AMP27 is the consolidated Annual Management Plan for 2027, developed to align LPPSA’s initiatives, programmes, and resource priorities with the HOME31 Strategic Framework.

It brings together departmental proposals into one integrated portfolio, ensuring that every initiative contributes clearly to LPPSA’s strategic pillars, corporate priorities, operational needs, and long-term transformation agenda.

AMP27 also serves as a management tool to strengthen prioritisation, eliminate duplication, improve budget discipline, clarify accountability, and monitor the delivery of initiatives throughout 2027.

Through AMP27, LPPSA moves from isolated departmental planning towards a coordinated, value-driven, and organisation-wide execution of HOME31.
